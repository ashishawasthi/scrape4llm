# Your Guide to Unexpected Moments

  


Life does not always go the way we would like. At times like these,   
we want to be as much help as we can.

[Access Emergency Funds for a Loved One who has Lost Mental Capacity](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/fjc)
    Applying to manage the bank account for your loved one who lost mental capacity takes time. In the meantime, should you need emergency funds for your loved one's needs, you can apply to make emergency funds withdrawal of up to S$5,000 from your loved one's account.
