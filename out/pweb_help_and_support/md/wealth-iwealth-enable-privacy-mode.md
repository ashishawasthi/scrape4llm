# Enable Privacy Mode to Mask Displayed Balances

Learn how to turn on Privacy mode to mask your displayed balances upon logging into DBS digibank.

  
  


Toggle easily between Privacy or Normal mode on the Home page of DBS digibank. Privacy mode will mask all balances in the summary page while Normal mode will show all balances.

To enable Privacy mode and mask your balances in the Home page, click on the **Privacy Mode** option on the upper right corner of DBS digibank Home page to set your preference:  
  


### Normal Mode

  
  


### Privacy Mode

  
  
To set Privacy mode as the default setting, under the icon, select **Settings** , then **Manage Display Preference**.
