# Portfolio Analysis

Monitor your portfolio performance and add benchmarks to compare.

  
  


  * [Log in to DBS digibank](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/iwealth) with your **User ID** and **PIN** , or login to the DBS digibank Mobile App with your **Touch / Face ID** or **User ID & PIN**.
  * Click on the **Wealth Management** tab or the **Investment Portfolios** tile.
  * Select the **Analysis** tab.
  * View analysis by Total Assets, Performance, Income & Analysis or Maturity by clicking on the individual tabs.
