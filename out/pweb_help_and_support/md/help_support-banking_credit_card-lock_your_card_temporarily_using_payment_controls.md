## Lock your Card Temporarily using Payment Controls

Our Payment Control features allows you to temporarily lock your credit/debit card if it's misplaced.

### How to Lock your Credit/Debit Card

  * [Credit Card](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/card-temp-lock-card.html#mobile-tab1)
  * [Debit Card](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/card-temp-lock-card.html#mobile-tab2)



**Step 1**  
Log in to digibank mobile with your Touch / Face ID or digibank User ID & PIN. 

**Step 2**  
Under Home, tap Cards and select the Card that you wish to lock. 

**Step 3**  
Select Payment Controls to manage your card. 

**Step 4**  
Toggle the Switch Off to temporarily lock your card. 

**Step 5**  
Tap Lock Now to proceed. 

**Step 6**  
Your card has been Locked.
